How It Works

    
This tool uses simplified models inspired by NASA's Sentry-II impact monitoring system and Probabilistic Asteroid Impact Risk (PAIR) assessments. Enter parameters to simulate entry, airburst, and potential damage. For real threats, visit NASA's CNEOS Sentry system.

    

      

        
Sentry-II Algorithm

        
Models thousands of orbital paths to compute impact probabilities as low as 1 in 10 million.

      

      

        
Monte Carlo Simulations

        
Evaluates millions of scenarios for blast waves, tsunamis, and global effects using supercomputing tools like Cart3D.

      

      

        
Risk Assessment

        
From local damage to planetary-scale hazards, anchored in NASA's ATAP project.
