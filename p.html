NASA Research & History: Asteroids

Exploring the origins, science, and missions to asteroids that shaped our solar system

Why Study Asteroids?

Asteroids are time capsules of the early solar system. They hold secrets about how planets formed, may contain resources like metals and water, and studying them helps us develop strategies to protect Earth from potential impacts.
